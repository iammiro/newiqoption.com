Our Business Model

Our business model is based on pure exchange rule - at any moment and for any price there are traderswilling to buy and there are traderswilling to sell.

In an ideal situation the positions of those who buy and of those who sell are equal.

Theclearing is done inside our system and we get our commission out of the trading volume.

In cases where there aredifferences between the buyers and the sellers we pass this risk to themarket maker which covers the risk up to $7000, this risk is also called“exposure”.

PROFIT OF UP TO85%

We give our traders an average of up to85% profit; that means that ourcommission is about7.5% from the inside clearing trading volume.

The market maker will get the same commission but in the long run. This will happen due to the fact that according to the law of large numbers the chances of winning and losing are the same.
